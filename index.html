About

Meet Barbara Zamost

A communications strategist with deep roots in the Bay Area, Barbara Zamost brings decades of expertise in shaping narratives for organizations that matter.

Barbara specializes in elevating mission-driven nonprofits and leading consumer products through strategic public relations and communications. Her approach combines rigorous media strategy with authentic storytelling — ensuring that every pitch, press release, and campaign reflects the true heart of an organization.

Based in the vibrant San Francisco Bay Area, she understands the regional media landscape intimately, while maintaining strong national relationships with journalists, editors, and producers across every major platform.
